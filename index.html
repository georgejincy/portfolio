ABOUT ME

Passionate. Creative. Hardworking

hi,

I'm a web developer based in North Carolina. I love creating memorable user experiences using simple modern designs. I love playing with colors, tinkering with designs and animations and I love to learn!

Previously, I worked as a RPG developer for IBM iSeries systems. I couldn't wait to work on applications that had color in them. When I learned about the Full Stack developer program at University of North Carolina - Chapel Hill, I knew the opportunity was perfect for me to step into the field of web development. I found that I could use my coding skills from my experience as a programmer with an added advantage - create whatever I can imagine! Now, there is no looking back.

I also love to draw and sketch during my freetime and all things DIY!

WATCH MY FARM - A farm sitting app

The WatchMyFarm application allows a farmer to log their animals, detailed feed and care instructions and schedule their time away. Once scheduled, the farmer then sends a link to the sitter to access the duties assigned and they can monitor the sitters progress of completing the duties.

TECHNOLOGIES

Frontend: React, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS
API: Dark Sky API
Back End: NodeJS, Express, Express Handlebars, PassportJS, ShortID, MySQL, Sequelize

TRAIN SCHEDULER

A train schedule application that incorporates Firebase to host arrival and departure data. The application retrieves and manipulate this information with Moment.js. It provides up-to-date information about various trains, namely their arrival times and how many minutes remain until they arrive at their station. The "minutes to arrival" and "next train time" are updated once every minute

TECHNOLOGIES

Frontend: Firebase, JavaScript, jQuery, MomentJS, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS
